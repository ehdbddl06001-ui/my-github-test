## 문제
An unvaccinated child has a **gray pseudomembrane** over the tonsils, a **bull
neck**, then **myocarditis with heart block**. The responsible toxin halts host
protein synthesis and shares its exact mechanism with **Pseudomonas aeruginosa
exotoxin A**. Which mechanism is it?

- A. ADP-ribosylation of elongation factor 2 (EF-2), arresting translation
- B. ADP-ribosylation of the Gs-alpha subunit, persistently raising cAMP
- C. Removal of an adenine from 28S rRNA of the 60S ribosomal subunit
- D. Cleavage of SNARE proteins, blocking neurotransmitter release
- E. Cross-linking of MHC class II and the T-cell receptor as a superantigen

## 정답 및 해설
> 정답: A

- **기전**: **Corynebacterium diphtheriae** 독소와 **Pseudomonas exotoxin A**는
  모두 **EF-2를 ADP-ribosylation**하여 불활성화 → **단백질 합성 정지** → 세포
  사멸. 임상: 인두 위막, 심근염(전도장애), 뇌신경/말초 신경병.
- **오답감별(독소 기전 비교)**:
  - (B) **Gs ADP-ribosylation → cAMP↑**: **콜레라 독소**, ETEC 이열성독소(LT),
    백일해는 **Gi 억제**로 cAMP↑.
  - (C) **28S rRNA에서 아데닌 제거(60S 불활성)**: **Shiga / Shiga-like(EHEC)**
    독소 → HUS.
  - (D) **SNARE 절단**: **보툴리눔**(ACh 방출 차단→이완마비), **파상풍**(glycine/
    GABA 방출 차단→경직마비).
  - (E) **초항원(superantigen)**: **TSST-1**, 연쇄상구균 발열외독소 → 다량
    사이토카인.
- **임상핵심**: "EF-2 ADP-ribosylation = 디프테리아 + Pseudomonas exotoxin A."
  치료/예방: 디프테리아 항독소 + 백신(toxoid).
